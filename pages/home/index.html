One Voice One Day

A 24-Hour Global Digital Gathering of Synchronized Worship & Prayer

About

While I was working with Firm Collective through Develop for Good as an Engineer Manager, the Firm Collective team asked us if there was anyone willing to help them with the launch of their new One Voice One Day event. PhysMatch

Connecting locals with their family physicians. Using EEG's to detect client's emotions.

About

I joined Canada's largest neuro-based hackathon by NeurAlberta creating a product for the problem solver division stream. The task was to get a problem from a unique expert in the field and come up with a viable solution which integrates neuro-tech. The problem we were given was that in Alberta, it is hard for locals to find a family physician near them. We had 2 months to research and prep before the 48-hour hackathon. We created a website that would allow locals to easily connect with family physicians, and added in a bonus feature where a client would be able to wear a BCI headset where the physician would be able to see the emotions of the wearer!
